Allameh English Institute

Allameh English language institute boasts the distinction of being first and foremost amongst other institutes to obtain the required license, issued by the Ministry of Education.Allameh laid its foundation in 1367 with the assistance of the most competent faculty of the time and ever since it has been proverbially regarded as an avant-garde institute in adopting the most innovative methods in teaching English presented in the world.
What primarily distinguishes Allameh is its assorted terms and levels devised in tune with the requirements of varied age groups with comprehensive stress on all skills in an environment most conducive to learning.
